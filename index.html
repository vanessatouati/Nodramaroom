How it works
Simple for you. Magic for students.
1
Students sign up with their .ac.il email
Auto-verified. No fake profiles. Only your students access matching for your dorms.
2
They build their lifestyle profile
12 questions: sleep schedule, kosher, cleanliness, gender preferences, study habits, stay duration.
3
NDR forms compatible groups of 3-4
Both for new dorms assignments AND replacements when someone leaves mid-year.
4
Your housing office gets a dashboard
Real-time KPIs: occupancy, satisfaction, conflict tickets, switch times. You see what's working.
Bonus features for your students
Services that come included.
🛋️
Furniture marketplace
Departing students sell to arriving ones — less waste, lower move-in costs.
💬
Group chat & voting
Roommates organize visits, chores, house rules through a structured group chat.
