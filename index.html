Good roommates, matched right.

A roommate-matching app for Israeli university students. Hybrid swipe — individuals or groups — across campus dorms (Broshim, Einstein, Student Village, Zweig, Federman, Talia…) and off-campus apartments. Verified by .ac.il email, filtered by lifestyle, kosher preferences, gender, and stay duration.
